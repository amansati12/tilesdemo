Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Can't find your answer? Our design team is just a call or WhatsApp away.

Ask on WhatsApp
Do you provide free tile samples?
Yes! We provide free physical tile samples for all our collections. You can request up to 3 samples per inquiry, delivered to your address anywhere in India within 5–7 working days. Simply fill in the contact form or WhatsApp us with your chosen tiles.
What are your minimum order quantities?
For retail clients, we supply from as few as 5 boxes (approximately 10 sqm). For commercial and builder clients, we offer better pricing on orders of 500 sqm and above. Contact our B2B team at projects@luminos.in for bulk pricing.
Do you ship across India? What about export?
We deliver to all major cities and towns across India via our logistics partners. We also export to 40+ countries — including UAE, UK, USA, Singapore, and East Africa. International shipping is available for orders of 1000 sqm and above.
Can I visit the showroom without an appointment?
Absolutely! Our showroom is open during listed hours and walk-in visitors are always welcome. For a more personalized experience with a dedicated design consultant and private viewing of exclusive collections, we recommend booking a consultation in advance.
Do you provide installation services?
While we don't directly provide installation, we maintain a curated network of certified tile installation professionals across major cities. Upon purchase, we can connect you with a vetted installer in your area, and provide detailed installation guidelines at no extra cost.
What warranty do your tiles come with?
All LUMINOS tiles come with a manufacturer's warranty against defects in material and finish. Porcelain and vitrified tiles carry a 10-year warranty; natural stone tiles carry a 5-year warranty. Our customer service team handles all warranty claims with priority resolution.
Can you create custom tile designs for my project?
Yes, for commercial and high-end residential projects, we offer custom design and manufacturing services. This includes bespoke sizes, custom colour glazes, and personalised patterns. Custom orders typically have a minimum of 500 sqm and a 8–12 week lead time. Contact our design team to discuss.
