Our Heritage

Born from a Passion for Stone

LUMINOS was founded in 1999 by Master Craftsman Vikram Shah, who believed that every floor and every wall deserved the dignity of fine art.

Starting from a small workshop in Ahmedabad's historic Design District, Vikram's obsession with texture, colour, and material led him to the quarries of Rajasthan, the marble mines of Italy, and the ceramic ateliers of Morocco. What he brought back was more than raw material — it was a philosophy: that luxury is not decoration, it is precision.

Today, LUMINOS is India's premier destination for luxury tiles, trusted by 1,200+ architects and interior designers, and present in homes, hotels, and commercial spaces across 40 countries.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the world's most trusted name in luxury stone and tile — a brand synonymous with artistry, integrity, and the relentless pursuit of perfection.

Our Mission

To connect artisans, designers, and homeowners through collections that transform every surface into a canvas — making extraordinary design accessible to all who value beauty.

Our Values

Craftsmanship over convenience. Honesty in material. Respect for nature. These three values guide every decision — from quarry to delivery to your home.
