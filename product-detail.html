Bianco Carrara is a masterful reinterpretation of the legendary Italian Carrara marble in durable, vitrified porcelain. With its crisp white base and delicate grey veining, it brings timeless elegance to residential and commercial flooring. The rectified edges ensure minimal grout joints for a seamless, gallery-like appearance.

Type: Vitrified Porcelain
Usage: Floor, Indoor
Surface: Polished / Matte
MOH Hardness: 7
Chemical Resistance: Class A
Frost Resistance: Yes
Breaking Strength: ≥1300 N
Rectified: Yes (±0.3mm)

Use high-quality white polymer cement adhesive. Ensure substrate is level (±2mm over 2m). Recommended grout joint: 1.5–3mm. Apply with notched trowel (8mm for 600×600, 10mm for larger formats). Seal grout after 72hrs curing. Use professional tile levelling system for large formats.

Routine: Clean with pH-neutral floor cleaner and warm water. Avoid acidic or bleach-based cleaners. Deep clean: Use porcelain tile cleaner monthly. Polish surfaces can be maintained with a microfibre mop. Avoid abrasive scrubbers. Stain resistance is excellent — act promptly on spills.
